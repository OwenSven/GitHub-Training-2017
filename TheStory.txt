Mr. Simons and The Squirrel: A Short Story

Once upon a time, there was a man who convinced the others to call him
Mr. Simons. He was a kind man, especially to those he did not despise. He
was especially kind to the love of his life: The Squirrel.

One day, Mr. Simons found the Squirrel dead.

Mr. Simons decided to put his friend to rest the only way he knew how. viking funeral style. he put him on a tiny boat, sent the boat off to sea, and shot a flaming arrow at the boat. Bily robin, the squirell, was immortized in the stars from that day forth.
